Jared Fisher

I'm a Ph.D. student studying Bayesian statistics and its applications to finance and business, under the guidance of my advisor, Carlos Carvalho. My program is called "Risk Analysis and Decision Making" in the Department of Information, Risk and Operations Management at the University of Texas at Austin McCombs School of Business.

My areas of research have included dynamic linear models, nonlinear models, and Bayesian nonparametric priors, applied in projects within the fields of finance, econometrics, healthcare and sports.
